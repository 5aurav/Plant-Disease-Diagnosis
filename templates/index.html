How It Works

Our AI-powered tool helps you identify plant diseases in three simple steps

1. Upload Image

Take a clear photo of your plant leaf or upload one.

2. AI Analysis

The model analyzes the image to detect plant disease.

3. Get Results

You receive a report with disease name and confidence.
